Welcome to 21st century fundraising

Money has changed. We're living in a tap-in, tap-out world. So Harness has created a series of seamlessly connected technologies that get your organisation in your donor's pocket, in a 21st century way - helping you make big things happen a little each day.

What can Harness do for you?

  • Collect

    Say goodbye to awkward cold calls and unopened direct mail. Collecting donations just got easier and faster than ever.

  • Engage

    Show your supporters the big difference they're making, as they're making it - and build a relationship that will last a lifetime.

  • Analyze

    Get to know your donors and how they're donating, with real-time analysis, insights, tracking and reports.

  • Customize

    Our white-label technology gives you complete control of your donor's experience, from the first page to the final click.
